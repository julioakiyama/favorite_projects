Short Biography

Since that first lesson in 1996, I have been in over 28 semi-professional regional shows. My roles have included Don Quixote (Man of LaMancha), Captain Hook (Peter Pan), Ben Rumson (Paint Your Wagon), Fagin (Oliver), Tin Man (Wizard of Oz), John Dickinson (1776), Duke (Big River) Danvers Carew (Jekyll and Hyde), and many others.

I have been a featured soloist on two CDs; Pilate on the CD "He Lives", an Easter Cantata by Dean Kaelin, and Jubal on the CD of the original cast of "The White Star" by Doug Stewart (Saturday's Warrior) and Janice Kapp Perry.
